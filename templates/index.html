Process

1. Users are able to navigate to the “Upload” page to submit an image for annotation.

2. Upon completion, the image can be found with all other images in the “Library” page or can be filtered through in the “Search” page.

3. Within the "Library" and "Search" page, users are able to preview and download the original images as well as the annotated versions.
